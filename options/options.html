Notion Integration

Connected
  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations and click "New integration".
  2. Pick a name (e.g. "My Bookmarks"), select your workspace, and submit.
  3. Copy the Internal Integration Secret token and paste it below.
  4. In Notion, open each database you want to save to → ... → Connections → add your integration.
